Fall Collection

Description

In this 2023 collection, we have carefully selected Lord of the Rings as our primary source of inspiration. By delving into this enchanting saga, we have curated a captivating array of six distinct aromas that effortlessly evoke the essence of this epic tale.

As the season transitions into autumn, we find ourselves mesmerized by the captivating spectacle of the changing foliage, allowing us to taste the invigorating crispness in the air. With each waft of our meticulously crafted products, our customers are transported to a time when strolling through the majestic woods was not merely a leisurely activity, but a mode of travel filled with wonder and adventure.

Each of our soap and candle products is lovingly and impeccably handcrafted in small, specialized batches to ensure the utmost quality and attention to detail.
