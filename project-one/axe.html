THE AXE

Packs quite a wallop!

The axe is a hard-hitting weapon thats a little slow, but deals a lot of damage to multiple enemies in an arc. Faster than hammers, but slower than daggers and gauntlets.
The hammer has a special attack where the axe can be thrown and deal damage in a line before coming back much like a boomerang.



  • Has a chance of poisoning enemies upon hitting, which deals damage every few seconds

  • Has a chance of healing the wielder using the vitality of felled enemies

  • Has a chance of spawning a ghost from a felled enemy that attacks nearby monsters

  • Fervor collected from felled enemies also increases inspiration

  • Has a chance of dealing a critical hit upon striking, increasing damage

  • Deals a lot more damage than mortal weapons
