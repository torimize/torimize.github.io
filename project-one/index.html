THE GAME

Cult of the Lamb is a cute and creepy rogue-like game developed by Massive Monster and Devolver Digital. The game follows The Lamb, a cute little lamb on a quest to start a cult in the name of their patron The One Who Waits and defeat all heretics standing in their way.
Includes a ton of fun mechanics such as a dungeon-crawl, resource gathering/management, relationship-building, base decorating, and lore collection.





THE WEAPONS

A big portion of Cult of the Lamb is the dungeon-crawl, where your character faces hordes of monsters and heretics. Of course, the Lamb's main source of damage is their weapons. The Lamb has access to weapon's such as:

  • Daggers
  • Axes
  • Hammers
  • Gauntlets
